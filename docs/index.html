NEUMORPHISM !

is a design trend that emerged in 2019 and is emerging as an alternative to minimalist design. Neumorphism is a design language that creates a sense of depth and lifelike appearance by using shadows and highlights to distinguish between objects and backgrounds.


Neumorphism style has the following characteristics:


  • Use of shadows and highlights :

    Neumorphism distinguishes between objects and backgrounds using shadows and highlights rather than color differences. This gives a round and soft impression

  • Simplicity and flexibility :

    Neumorphism maintains the simplicity of minimalist design, while adding flexibility and depth through the use of shadows and highlights.

  • Tactile experience :

    Neumorphism provides a tactile experience that makes UI more than just graphics. This increases user immersion.


Neumorphism is applied in a variety of fields. In particular, it is gaining popularity in mobile apps, websites, and UI design.



Advantages of Neumorphism:


  • Visual appeal:
    Neumorphism catches the user's eye with its three-dimensional and lively design.
  • Usability:
    Neumorphism has excellent usability with its simple and intuitive design.
  • Appeal:
    Neumorphism is popular with users because it gives a friendly and comfortable feeling.

Disadvantages of Neumorphism:


  • Difficulty of implementation:
    Neumorphism can be difficult to implement because it expresses depth through
  • Restriction on content:
    Neumorphism can simplify content for a three-dimensional design.

Neumorphism is still a new design trend, so it remains to be seen how it will evolve in the future.
